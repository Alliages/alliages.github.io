A pioneer in the field of urban environmental thinking, the City of Paris has carried out a strategic study to establish a carbon free roadmap for Paris 2050 ahead of the upcoming revision of its Climat Plan initiative.


Elioth, an entity of Egis engineering group, are specialised in R+D and in the design of low carbon innovation. Elioth are very proud to have led the study group with partners Conseil by Egis, Quattrolibri (low carbon strategies) and Mana (consulting group specialised in sociology). Our studies allowed the City of Paris to access an independent and impartial external point of view which took into account the vast array of problems which were to be taken into account. The teams’ proposed measures should allow Paris to open the debate towards a low carbon transition as part of a vision which is shared by every one of its residents.
